What are quality statements?

Quality statements use the best available evidence to identify priority areas for quality improvement. They focus on areas where variation in care has been identified.

Each statement describes what high-quality care looks like. They provide a benchmark for care and a framework for measuring improvements.

The statements come from our collection of quality standards. Find out how we develop them, how you can get involved and what topics are coming up.

Benefits of using NICE quality statements

Support

Know what high-quality care looks like when providing or commissioning services.

Measure

Use metrics that are nationally-recognised, robust, evidenced-based and consistent.

Improve

Find opportunities for quality improvement and set your strategy for the year ahead.
