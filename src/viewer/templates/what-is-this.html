About NICE Quality Statements

Get started

  • Filter through of all the published quality statements relevant to your particular service, setting or population of interest from across the whole quality standards programme.
  • Use one or more of the filters to narrow down your search; you can expand or narrow your search further at any stage.
  • Select any of the results in the list to see the full statement, associated measures and useful related information.

Who are they for?

  • Commissioners to inform new service specification.
  • Service providers to benchmark their current service provision.
  • Commissioners and service providers together to help prioritise key areas for service improvement activities.

Benefits of using NICE Quality Statements

Support

Quality statements support commissioners and service providers with benchmarking and quality improvement initiatives.

Measure

Each NICE quality statement describes what high quality care or service provision looks like for a specific area of care, and provides measures to support initial benchmarking and evaluation of improvement.

Improve

Statements exist for those areas of care identified through the NICE quality standards programme as a high priority for quality improvement.
